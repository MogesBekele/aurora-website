Product

Medical Oxygen

Medical oxygen is essential in hospital and clinical care for resuscitation and surgery and for various therapies. Oxygen is vital to combat pneumonia and neonatal related under five children mortality and morbidity, for treatment of many obstetric emergencies, including cardiac arrest, acute blood loss, shock, dyspnea (breathlessness), pulmonary edema, unconsciousness, convulsions (eclampsia), and fetal distress during labor. A respiratory disease like COVID-19 which affects our lungs and can cause the oxygen level to drop to dangerous levels, we would need to undergo what is known as oxygen therapy. In line with this we are happy that ACOM was one of the main suppliers of medical oxygen in Ethiopia to hospitals, COVID-19 care centers and clinics during the peak of the pandemic and afterwards.
